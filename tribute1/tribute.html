Martin Luther King Jr

American Minister, Activist And Political Philosopher

Here's a timeline of Dr, Martin Luther King Jr:

  • 1929 - Born in Atlanta Georgia, United State.
  • 1935 - At age 6 King Jr Attended the David T.Howard Elementary School in Atlanta
  • 1942 - When King Jr was 13, he became the youngest assistant manager of a newspaper delivery Station for the Atlanta Journal.
  • 1948 - King graduated from Morehouse with a Bachelor of Arts in sociology, aged ninteen(19)
  • 1953 - While still studying in Boston Universtiy, King married Dexter Scott on the lawn of her parents' house, in Heiberger, Alabama.[
  • 1954 - At the age of 25,King was called as pastor of the Dexter Avenue Baptist Church in Montgomery, Alabama.
  • 1955 - Dr.Martin Luther King was arrested on this day for the first time in his civil rights career.
  • 1958 - King survived a Knife Attact
  • 1959 - King served as co-pastor with his father at the Ebenzer Baptist Church till his death
  • 1963 - King lead the 1963 march in Washington, where he delivered his "I Have A Dream" Speech
  • 1964 - The FBI mailed King a threatning anonymous letter
  • 1964 - King won the Nobel Peace Prize for combating racial inequality through non violent resistance.
  • 1965 - King got arrested again for leading more than 250 activists to the Dallas County Courthouse to register their vote. They were charged with 'parading without a permit'.
  • 1968 - Martin Luther King was shot dead while standing on a balcony outside his second-floor room at the Lorraine Motel in Memphis, Tennessee.
